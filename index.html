FAQ

Is Aurox custodial?

No. Funds are held in non-custodial smart contracts you can verify. You can withdraw anytime subject to strategy cooldowns.

Where do yields come from?

From lending/borrowing incentives and staking rewards on protocols such as Navi & Scallop. Strategies prioritize risk-adjusted return.

What fees do I pay?

A flat 5% performance fee on realized profits. No deposit or management fees.
